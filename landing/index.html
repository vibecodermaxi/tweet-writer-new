The Problem with AI Content

Most AI writing tools optimize for speed. You get a 1,000-word article in 30 seconds, but it reads like... AI wrote it. Generic openings, hedge words everywhere, no unique angle, claims without data.

Draper Writer takes a different approach: write one piece at a time, critique it honestly, and iterate until it's actually good.

The Quality Loop

1

Gather Requirements

Type, audience, goal, tone, word count

2

Research

Web search for data, angles, and real examples

3

Write Draft

Create version with research incorporated

4

Critique

Honest self-evaluation against 7-point checklist

5

Iterate or Publish

Score below threshold? Fix the weakest criterion. Otherwise, ship it.

The 7-Point Quality Checklist

Hook stops the scroll - First 2-3 sentences are compelling
Unique angle - Fresh perspective, not a generic take
Claims backed by data - Statistics, examples, or real experience
Sounds human - No AI patterns or excessive hedging
Actionable - Reader learns something they can apply
Meets word count - Within the target range
Matches tone - Voice matches what was requested
